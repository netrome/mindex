# Language Notes

## Goals
- Learn key phrases for greetings and transit
- Practice reading signs and menus

## Strategy
- Write a small phrase list per country
- Focus on polite basics and numbers

## Phrase Bank
| Phrase | Meaning | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| hello | greeting | say it like you mean it |
| where is the station | transit help | point, then ask |
| please and thanks | politeness | use both, always |
| open sesame | secret word | easter egg, do not use |

## Cheatsheet
- Keep a tiny list in your pocket notebook
- Practice `one` to `ten` for prices
- Practice `yes` and `no` so you can say both clearly
