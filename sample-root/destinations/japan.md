# Japan

## Tokyo
- Neighborhood based days: one area per day
- Set aside time for a short day trip

## Kyoto
- Visit early temples before crowds
- Include a quiet evening walk

> Night walks reveal the best vending machine surprises.

## Day Trips
- Nikko for forests and shrines
- Kamakura for the coast
- If weather turns:
  - indoor museum day
  - tea ceremony workshop

## Mini Checklist
- [ ] Suica or Pasmo card
- [ ] Cash for smaller shops
- [ ] Reserve a tiny jazz bar
